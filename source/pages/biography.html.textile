James Mead :: floehopper.com

After a couple of years as an electronic engineer on "an Antarctic research station":https://www.bas.ac.uk/about/about-bas/our-history/british-research-stations-and-refuges/faraday-f/, James has spent more than a decade developing software at various "consultancies and startups":http://www.linkedin.com/in/jamesmead#experience in the UK and US. After a stint at "Thoughtworks":http://www.thoughtworks.com/, he was the first employee at "Reevoo":http://www.reevoo.com/, an early adopter of "Ruby on Rails":http://rubyonrails.org/. Since going freelance earlier this year, he helped develop the website for Antony Gormley's "One & Other":http://www.oneandother.co.uk/ project. He's the author of "Mocha":https://mocha.jamesmead.org/, a popular Ruby testing framework.
