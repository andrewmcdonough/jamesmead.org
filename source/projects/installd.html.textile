A social network for owners of iPhone apps. The idea was to discover useful apps used/recommended by your friends. A "RubyCocoa":https://github.com/rubycocoa/rubycocoa/ Mac desktop system tray app scanned the relevant iTunes directory and updated your list of apps on the server.

* "Server repo":https://github.com/floehopper/installd
* "Client repo":https://github.com/floehopper/installd-client
